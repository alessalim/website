Ingredients:


  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips


    • Instructions:

    1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a baking pan.
    2. Melt the butter in a microwave or on the stovetop. In a mixing bowl, combine the melted butter, sugar, cocoa powder, and salt.
    3. Beat in the eggs and vanilla extract until the mixture is smooth.
    4. Gently fold in the flour until just combined. Stir in the chocolate chips.
    5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
    6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
    7. Allow the brownies to cool in the pan before cutting them into squares.
    8. Enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate brownies!
